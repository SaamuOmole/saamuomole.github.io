UN Datathon (RISING)

Focus: Geospatial data + ML to support SDG-related investment decisions.

Participated in a 72-hour long 2023 UN Datathon competition. It is an annual event organised by the United Nations Committee of Experts on Big Data and Data Science for Official Statistics (UNCEBD). This represented an opportunity to contribute solutions to the challenges of the Sustainable Development Goals, which aligns with my passion about sustainability. Entered the competition as part of a team of 4.

The UN introduced 6 key transitions , supported by 5 enablers, to accelerate progress towards achieving achieving the SDGs by 2030. We were tasked with creating an innovative data solution which tackles local sustainable development challenges, and which leverages one or several of the six transitions . The end product should be a solution that combines geospatial data to advance the SGDs.

Our project was called Regional Investment Sustainably Increasing National GDP (RISING). It explored Kenya's GDP by county. It aims to help government and aid organizations maximize the impact of their investments by enabling them to understand which investments of the sustainable development pathways would also increase the GDP of a region. The analysis and mdoeling was done at the regional level to understand the different factors that are important for the different regions. This effort is in line with the theme of that edition of the competition, which is about localising actions and monitoring efforts.

I was in charge of collecting and combining open source geospatial datasets with other data from various verifiable sources. The datasets encompass 4 different sustainable development pathways that we identified namely, energy access, biodiversity loss, food systems and digital connectivity. I also took the lead on the modelling side, where we built a machine learning model to estimate the GDP per capita from inputs including energy, economic and geospatial features. The solution was deployed as an application on Streamlit which users can interact with.

What I did

  • Collected and harmonized open geospatial datasets with other data sources.
  • Led modeling to estimate the GDP per capita using economic + geospatial features.
  • Helped deploy an interactive Streamlit app.
