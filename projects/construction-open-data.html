Construction Open Data Project (Write up in progress)

Focus: House price prediction across UK regions based on diverse socio-economic features.

This is a project to predict house prices in different parts of the UK using diverse interpretable features e.g., construction, geographical land use, unemployment, population, interest rates, etc. The UK regions where house prices were analysed include North East, North West, Yorkshire & the Humber, East Midlands, West Midlands, East, London, South East and South West.

The various factors which affect house prices were investigated by analysis of the dataset and the machine learning model, which learns the underlying patterns between the different factors and the house price. The degree of influence of respective features on house price

I collaborated with a few team mates to work on this exploratory project. I was involved in the project end to end, from data sourcing, pre-processing, feature engineering, modelling and deployment. A streamlit app was created to package the solution for deployment.
